ABOUT US
Welcome to the Yale School of Art, a dynamic and innovative hub where creativity flourishes and artistic talents are nurtured. For over 150 years, our institution has been at the forefront of art education, fostering an environment where students are encouraged to explore, experiment, and excel in their chosen disciplines.
More Details

Welcome to the YALE SCHOOL OF ART,

Established in 1869, our school has a rich history of nurturing talented artists and fostering an environment where creative expression thrives. As one of the leading art schools in the world, we are dedicated to providing an unparalleled education that equips our students with the skills, knowledge, and critical thinking necessary to excel in the ever-evolving field of art.

We believe that art has the power to transform individuals and societies. Our programs are designed to challenge and inspire, encouraging students to push the boundaries of their creativity and explore new artistic horizons. Our esteemed faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and vibrant community of artists create an environment where innovation and collaboration are at the forefront.

Our alumni have gone on to make significant contributions to the art world, and their success is a testament to the quality of education and support they received at Yale. Whether you are a prospective student, a current student, or a member of our alumni network, we invite you to explore what the Yale School of Art has to offer and join us in our mission to shape the future of art.

At the Yale School of Art, we pride ourselves on fostering a supportive and inclusive community. Our students come from diverse backgrounds and bring unique perspectives that enrich the learning experience. Collaboration and dialogue are at the heart of our approach, and we encourage our students to engage with one another and the broader artistic community.

Our mission is to create an environment where art can thrive, where students are encouraged to take risks, and where the pursuit of artistic excellence is a shared goal.

If you are passionate about art and ready to take your skills to the next level, the Yale School of Art is the place for you. Join us in our mission to push the boundaries of creativity and make a lasting impact on the world through art.

OUR PROGRAMS

GRADUATE

The Yale School of Art's graduate program offers an immersive and rigorous experience for emerging artists. Our MFA program provides specialized training in painting/printmaking, sculpture, photography, and graphic design. Students are encouraged to push their creative boundaries and develop their unique artistic voice.

More Details

UNDERGRADUATE

Undergraduates at Yale College have the opportunity to engage with our faculty and facilities through elective courses and the art major. These programs allow students to explore various mediums, build foundational skills, and integrate their artistic practice with a liberal arts education.
