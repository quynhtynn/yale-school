MEET OUR FACULTY + -

Welcome to the Yale School of Art's Faculty Page, where you can get to know the accomplished artists, scholars, and educators who make up our esteemed faculty. With diverse expertise and a passion for teaching, our faculty members are dedicated to mentoring the next generation of artists and shaping the future of the art world.
